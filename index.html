📋 Guide

How to Buy

Simple steps to claim your rank or cosmetic. Process is fast — usually within a few minutes!

1
Choose Your Rank
Browse the ranks or cosmetics above and pick what you want to purchase.
2
Scan the QR
Go to the Payment section, select your item, and scan the GCash QR code to pay the exact amount.
3
Screenshot Proof
Take a screenshot of your successful payment — this is your proof of purchase!
4
Post on Discord
Post your screenshot in the #tambayan-store channel on Discord with your in-game username.
5
Bedrock Players
Playing on Bedrock / MCPE? Add * before your username when posting your proof.
6
Enjoy!
